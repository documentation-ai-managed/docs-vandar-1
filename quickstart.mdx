## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address
- Access to a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari recommended)
- A payment method (credit card, digital wallet, or bank transfer)
</Callout>

Vandar makes it easy to get started with purchasing digital game accounts and PC titles. Follow these steps to create an account and make your first purchase.

## Create Your Account

Sign up in under a minute to access exclusive deals on popular games like Elden Ring and EA Sports FC.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Vandar" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [vandarstore.com](https://vandarstore.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Locate the "Sign Up" or "تسجيل" button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a password, and confirm. Accept the terms of service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your login credentials securely. Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings for added security.
</Callout>

## Browse and Search Products

Discover top-selling items like ARK Survival Evolved or Ghost of Tsushima.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use Search Bar" icon="search">
    Enter keywords like "Elden Ring" or "فيفا 26" in the search field at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Results" icon="filter">
    Use filters for platform (PC), price range, or discounts (`<50%` off).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Categories" icon="layers">
    Browse sections like "الأكثر مبيعًا" (Best Sellers) for curated recommendations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add to Cart and Checkout

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop" icon="monitor">
    Click "أضف للسلة" (Add to Cart) on any product card, such as [Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree](https://vandarstore.com/ar/oZOaygl).
    
    Review your cart via the cart icon, adjust quantities, and proceed to checkout.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile" icon="smartphone">
    Tap the product image or "Add to Cart" button. Swipe to cart from the bottom navigation and select checkout.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Select Payment and Confirm Order

At checkout, choose your preferred method and complete the purchase.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Payment Options" icon="credit-card" horizontal>
    - Credit/Debit Cards
    - Apple Pay / Google Pay
    - Bank Transfer (local options)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order Confirmation" icon="check-circle">
    Enter billing details, review totals (e.g., 12.78 SAR for Elden Ring), and submit. Receive instant email confirmation and account delivery details.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Callout kind="success">
Orders are processed instantly for digital delivery. Track status in your dashboard at [vandarstore.com](https://vandarstore.com).
</Callout>
