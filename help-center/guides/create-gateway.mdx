## Overview
Create a payment gateway to accept online payments via Vandar.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Dashboard">
    Go to `https://panel.vandar.io` and login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Gateways">
    Click **Gateways** in the menu. Then, select **Create New Gateway**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter gateway name, callback URL, and any other required fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save gateway">
    Click **Create**. Your gateway will be ready to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>
