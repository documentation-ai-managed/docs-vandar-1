## Product Types

Vandar offers two main account types to suit your needs: shared accounts for budget options and private accounts for exclusive use.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Shared Accounts" icon="users">
    Ideal for casual gamers. Multiple users access the account under shared terms.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Perfect for trying games before committing to private access.
    </Callout>

    **Pros:**
    - Lower cost
    - Instant multi-platform support (e.g., Steam, PC)

    **Example Products:**
    | Product | Platform | Price |
    |---------|----------|-------|
    | Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree | PC | `<12.78` SAR |
    | Ghost of Tsushima | PC | Discounted |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Private Accounts" icon="user">
    Exclusive access for one user. Full ownership and privacy.
    
    **Pros:**
    - No sharing restrictions
    - Online Steam accounts ready to play

    **Example Products:**
    | Product | Platform | Original | Discounted |
    |---------|----------|----------|------------|
    | EA Sports FC 26 Ultimate | PC | 379 SAR | `<189.78` SAR |
    | ARK Survival Evolved | PC | 32.95 SAR | `<16.78` SAR |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
